Pancakes

Description

A crowd-pleasing banana pacake recipie that takes you from scratch to yum in 15 minutes. Prep time: 5mins Cook time: 10mins Servings: 6 Yield: 12 pancakes.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  2. 1 tablespoon white sugar
  3. 2 teaspoons baking powder
  4. quater teaspoon salt
  5. 1 beaten egg
  6. 1 cup of milk
  7. 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  8. 2 mashed ripe bananas
